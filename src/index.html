Simple Card

Cards are a convenient means of displaying content composed of different types of objects.

Here`s the usage:

<div class="tk-card"> <p class="tk-card__description"> Cards are a convenient means of displaying content composed of different types of objects. </p> <div class="tk-card__footer"> <button class="tk-button tk-button_narrow">MORE INFO…</button> </div> </div>
